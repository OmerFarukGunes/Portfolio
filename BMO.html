What Is This Project?

It is a project we are developing for BMO under the mentorship of BMO Adana representative Mr. Cantekin Güneşer. Our team consists entirely of students. Our main goal is to create a portal for computer engineers where they can find internships and jobs. Thus, team members can improve themselves in blazor, sql and front-end web dev.
