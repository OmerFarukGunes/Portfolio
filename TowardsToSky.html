What is Toward To Sky?

It's created by me when I was in 2nd grade. All textures was either redesigned or made from scratch. Also, sounds is Non-copy directly, arrangements were made on them. Your purpose is that make the highest score you can and collect the coins for upgrade your bonus content. I improved myself in Unity and c# with this project.
